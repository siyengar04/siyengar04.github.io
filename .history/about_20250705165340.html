about me

I'm a student, entrepreneur, and engineer excited to solve global issues through innovation and collaboration who promotes creativity, collectivism, and innovation through initiative. I have extensive experience with technology, including hardware engineering, electrical engineering, web development, and machine learning. I've led numerous projects and initiatives, some of which to international acclaim. In the future, I hope to continue my efforts to solve issues on a global scale with like-minded individuals! Currently, I'm helping out with research at UIUC's AUVSL (Autonomous and Unmanned Vehicle Systems Laboratory). My research topics include electromechanical design as well as vehicle dynamics simulation. I'm also experienced with both ROS1 and ROS2. In my free time, I enjoy robotics as a hobby, as well as film photography. I often take my own photos, developing and scanning them myself.
